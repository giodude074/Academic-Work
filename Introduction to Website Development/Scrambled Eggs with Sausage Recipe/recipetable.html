Scrambled Eggs with Sausage

IngredientsDirections
This simple recipe will make about 2 servings. This is handy for when you are on the go and don't feel like making anything too clomplicated. It is also yummy. Enjoy!
2 eggs sausage
  1. Cut up the sausages into slices.
  2. Heat up the frying pan and pour the butter onto in.
  3. Throw in the slices of sausage let it cook in low heat.
  4. Now beat the eggs, salt, and pepper into a bowl and mix until its well blended.
  5. Pour egg mixture into the pan with the sausages
  6. With a spatula mix up the eggs and the sausages together until you think its ready.
  7. Careful not to over cook the sausage in the process.
½ cup of butter
A pinch of salt pepper
